Ready to Begin Your Animation Project?

Where should I start?

Check out our portfolio to get a good understanding of what we can do. Fill out this form to start, and our representative will contact you within 24 hours. You can also call us or leave a message at 845 244-4888.

What should I prepare?

To kick things off smoothly, it'd be great if you could share your goals and expectations right from the start. If you have a budget in mind, feel free also to disclose it upfront so we can customize our approach and find the perfect solutions to fit your needs.

How much of a budget do I need?

We have three packages: Advance, Premium, and Ultimate. Your choice will depend on your specific requirements, goals, and budget constraints. Learn more about our packages here.

How long does a project take?

Most short animations will be completed in 25 business days. But, a more complex video project, like the one that utilizes our Ultimate package, can take longer as we have to add more details. If you're working against the clock, you can discuss with our team to figure out the best way forward with our service.

What are the usual payment terms?

Our typical payment terms involve an initial upfront payment to get the ball rolling, followed by milestone payments at key project stages. Depending on the size of your project, this payment term can be tailored to suit your needs.

Can I be hands-on during the production process?

Absolutely! We'll keep you in the loop at every step of the way and welcome your feedback to make sure that the final result aligns seamlessly with your vision.
